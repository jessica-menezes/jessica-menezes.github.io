About me

I'm a UX Designer who is passionate about creating user-centered designs! In particular, the research phase is one that really intrigues me as its the foundation to all other stages in the UX design development and key for building rapport with users. It allows me to remove any unconscious bias helping me to create a unique product for them. Providing users with a product that exceeds their expectations is what motivates me to think outside the box and bring my unique ideas to life.

My background in Electronic and Communications Engineering is where I developed an analytical approach to problem solving and sharpened my skills in coding, communication and decision making. These skills have allowed me to anticipate and manage users expectations. When I'm not designing, you'll find me cooking up a storm in the kitchen. My experience as a social media content creator has taught me how to innovate and produce content that is relevant and tailored not only to my followers but also to the social media sites I use. I’ve had the opportunity to work alongside many reputable brands such as Panasonic, Tilda, Zara Home, Mars Inc (Galaxy) and Lee Kum Kee.
